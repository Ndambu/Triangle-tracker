Triangle Tracker

This program helps us to track our triangles.

What is a triangle? A triangle is basically a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in Geometry.

We are going to look at three types of triangles;

  • An equilateral triangle
  • An isosceles triangle
  • A scalene triangle

Equilateral Triangle

This is a triangle that has three equal sides.

Isosceles Triangle

This is a triangle that has two equal sides.

Scalene Triangle

This is a triangle that has three unequal sides.
